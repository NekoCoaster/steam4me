Passenger Carriages for MSTS (Australia)

Menu Details

All passenger carriages (and DMUs) available on the site are listed here, arranged by letter code only, ie BE, U, etc without regard to the original owner of the vehicle. Vehicles by James Brook are also now hosted here with the generous permission of James Brook.

Carriages with a cyan font are PAYWARE models by team-ALCO hosted elsewhere; click on the usual link to go to the model's page on the team-ALCO site.
