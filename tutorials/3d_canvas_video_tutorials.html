Video Tutorials: Building A Loco With 3D Canvas
by Nick Fletcher

Overview

Nick has completed a series of video tutorials designed to help beginners get into building 3D models, particularly MSTS models, with 3D Canvas.

Below is a listing of the guides (hosted on YouTube) as well as links to download ZIP files containing the tutorials as AVI files. At the bottom of this page is a link to enable you to download the whole pack of tutorials (all four parts - a total of 20 videos) as a single ZIP file.

The tutorials can also be found at Nick's site at http://www.bandofgreen.com/videotutorials/
