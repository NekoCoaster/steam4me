930 Class Alco "World Series" Diesel (SA)

original 3d model design by Diego Rivera
Textures by Richard Lauder
Physics, sound set integration by team-ALCO

Overview

Twenty-two models in package: one each cab-end leading and one number two end (flat end) leading.

  • Tuscan and silver with Piping Shrike Logo;
  • Tuscan and silver with ANR logo;
  • Tuscan and silver with ANR logo and yellow bogies;
  • AN Green & gold.

These models contain enhanced friction, braking and resistance parameters by team-ALCO, specifically by Ian Bowles.

These locomotives have been designed for use with the LightFx lighting enhancement. The installer will install this enhancement for you. Click on this image for more details.
