West Coast Railways Diesel Fleet (VIC)

original 3d model design and textures by James Brook
West Coast Livery by Richard Lauder and Yuri Sos
Murraylander, V/Line Passenger Liveries by Yuri Sos & Richard Lauder

Overview

Package contains twelve locomotives:

  1. B class locomotives:
    • B61, B65, B76 in West Coast livery;
    • B80 in Murraylander and modified Murraylander livery;
  2. S class locomotives:
    • S300, S302, S311 in West Coast livery;
    • S302 ex-WCR in V/Line Passenger livery;
  3. T class locomotives:
    • T363, T369, T385 in West Coast livery

Logos and trademarks used with the express permission and generous support of the late Gary MacDonald, CEO and co-owner of the former West Coast Railways.

These locomotives have been designed for use with the LightFx lighting enhancement. The installer will install this enhancement for you. Click on this image for more details.
