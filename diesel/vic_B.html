Overview

Packages contain:

  1. As delivered - B60, B74 in blue and gold Victorian Railways livery;
  2. Modernised - B72 in blue and gold Victorian Railways livery, 83 in orange and grey V/Line livery;
  3. Experimental "tangerine-all-over" livery applied to B80.
