High Cab T Class Diesel Locomotive (VIC)

original 3d model design and textures by James Brook

Overview

Packages contain:

  1. T357 as delivered: freshly painted VR livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
  2. T349, T356 (second order):freshly painted VR livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
  3. T347, T353 (second order): weathered VR livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
  4. T365: (1960s version): freshly painted VR livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
  5. T358, T359: (with modified valance): freshly painted VR livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
  6. T364: freshly painted V/Line livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
  7. T363: weathered V/Line livery in both long-end and short-end leading versions;
     
  8. T363: in West Coast Railway livery: included in West Coast Railway Loco Pack.
These locomotives have been designed for use with the LightFx lighting enhancement. The installer will install this enhancement for you. Click on this image for more details.
