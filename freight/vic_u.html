Installation Instructions

  1. Download the installer to your PC. Run the installer; it will find your MSTS installation.
  2. You will need to have downloaded and installed the AU Victorian 4wheel sound set. This sound set is available from the SOUNDS page.
  3. You will then need to create a consist to use these wagons. Click here for a tutorial on building up your own consists.
